# Dottie's Milltown Frolic

A retro NES-style HTML5 canvas game series where you play as **Dottie the dog**, exploring the streets of **Milltown, NJ** on a daring off-leash adventure!

## Play Online

**[Play Dottie's Milltown Frolic!](https://ssmoke21.github.io/dotties-milltown-frolic/)** — pick between the NEW Frolic 2 and the classic original.

No install or build step required — each game is a single HTML file you can also open locally in any modern browser.

## 🌟 NEW: Frolic 2 — The Deluxe Adventure

`dotties-milltown-frolic-2.html`

The all-new sequel with better graphics and gameplay:

- **Hand-crafted pixel-art Milltown** — a fully drawn retro town (streets, houses, trees, Mill Pond, Lawrence Brook) modeled on the real street layout
- **Delivery missions** — fetch a pizza from Maria's and deliver it to Bestie's house! A quest arrow guides the way; complete deliveries to level up
- **Power-ups** — 👻 invisibility, 🧲 treat magnet, ⭐ super-star speed
- **New hazards** — cars cruising the streets (BONK!), and a dog catcher who shows up at level 3
- **Squirrels to chase** for bonus points
- **Day/night cycle** with glowing streetlamps and car headlights, plus occasional rain (parents can't see as far in the dark!)
- **Chiptune music & sound effects** — generated live with WebAudio, no files needed (press `M` to mute)
- **Family-friendly tuning** — gentle early levels, brief invincibility after getting caught
- Smooth camera, animated sprites, minimap, mobile touch controls

## Classic (v8)

`dotties-milltown-frolic-v8.html` (v7 also included)

The original frolic on real OpenStreetMap tiles of Milltown at zoom 16, with 13 GPS-accurate landmarks, minimap, high scores, and mobile touch controls.

## How to Play

- **Arrow Keys / WASD** — Move Dottie around town
- **Space** — Speed boost (collect 🦴 bones to recharge instantly)
- **P / Escape** — Pause
- **M** — Mute (Frolic 2)
- **Mobile** — On-screen D-pad + boost button

## Objective

Roam Milltown collecting treats from real local landmarks — Maria's Pizza, the Ice Cream Depot, Four Boys Ice Cream, Milltown Diner, and more — while avoiding Mom and Dad who want to bring you home. In Frolic 2, complete delivery missions to level up, grab power-ups, chase squirrels, and watch out for cars and the dog catcher!
