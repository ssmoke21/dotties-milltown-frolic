# Dottie's Milltown Frolic

A retro NES-style HTML5 canvas game where you play as **Dottie the dog**, exploring the real streets of **Milltown, NJ** on a daring off-leash adventure!

## Play

Open `dotties-milltown-frolic-v7.html` in any modern browser. No install or build step required.

## How to Play

- **Arrow Keys / WASD** — Move Dottie around the map
- **Space** — Activate speed boost (cooldown timer in HUD)
- **P / Escape** — Pause the game

## Objective

Roam Milltown collecting treats from real local landmarks — Maria's Pizza, the Ice Cream Depot, Four Boys Ice Cream, Milltown Diner, and more — while avoiding Mom and Dad who want to bring you home. Collect bones scattered around town to recharge your speed boost.

## Features

- Real OpenStreetMap tiles of Milltown, NJ at zoom level 16
- 13 real landmarks with GPS-accurate placement
- Pixel-art sprites with smooth animation
- Increasing difficulty across levels (parents get faster and more alert)
- Local high score leaderboard (top 5, stored in localStorage)
- Minimap with camera and entity tracking
- Screen shake and floating score particles
